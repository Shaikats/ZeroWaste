About Us

At ZeroWaste, we are committed to reducing food waste and fighting hunger by connecting those with surplus food to those in need. Our platform makes food donation easy, helping ensure that every meal counts and every donation makes a difference.

Our Mission

ZeroWaste aims to create a sustainable future by helping eliminate food waste, support communities in need, and foster responsible sharing practices. Together, we can make a difference.
